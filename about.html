About

It's been 7 years since I hung up by Tractor Battery selling hat and jumped into the world of tech with both feet.
Back in 2013 I was what you would call an amature geek. I had built my own PC with the help of friends but had very little experience of working in the tech sector unil I joined a young startup called Indeispring.

With the freedom to pursue my own interests, and a positive, excited young team to work alongside I soon discovered my talents for Design and UX. Being a small company with only 4 employees, everyone wore multiple hats, and aside from Design, I was also the account manager and Project Manager for the first few years.

As we grew and took on more people, we were able to specialise in our chosen fields. I first branched out into Project Management and UX Design before finally being able to focus entirely on UX and Design at the start of 2020.

Then COVID hit...

Placed on furlough from July, and with very little new design work coming in, It looked as though no sooner had I acheived my dream of being a full time creative designer that it would be snatched away by a cruel twist of fate.
However, with every crisis comes oportunity and so I founded Bartleby Design to take my work freelance and continue pursuing the dream.

That's enough about me though. Tell me more about yourself.
